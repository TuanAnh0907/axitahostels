About Us

We're a comfortable hostel located in the beautiful city of Bangalore. Here’s a little more about us below.

Who we are? Why choose us?

Based in the beautiful city of gardens – Bangalore, we’re a Boys and Girls Students Hostels that goes beyond providing just accommodation and safety. We aim to provide a living experience focusing on college students where color, caste or religion do not form barriers to form bonds, learn and live together in harmony.

The warm and friendly environment that we provide will make you feel at home. Situated in a residential area which is peaceful, we are located at a walking distance from local supermarkets, ATMs, shops and restaurants. Our staff is super friendly and will always lend a helping hand whenever you need.
